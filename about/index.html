Biography

My name is Victor Li Chuang. I completed my PhD in Computer Science from the National University of Singapore (Integrative Sciences and Engineering Programme) in 2025 under the co-supervision of Prof. Kan Min-Yen and Prof. Li Haizhou. Prior to the PhD, I finished my B.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering under the supervision of A/Prof Thomas Yeo

My research interests include Conversational Recommendation Systems and Interactive Dialogue Systems.
